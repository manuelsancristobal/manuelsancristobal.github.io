Following last week's session, I went with *The Logistics Lead* to brainstorm some new ideas, and the results were awesome! Within minutes, he provided a flood of creative ideas. This served as a reminder: *energy is contagious*. 

We decided to alter the dynamic by playing a little game: *"Guess Who?"*. I provided several famous characters, and the group had a blast asking and guessing. It was engaging, interactive, and precisely what the project required at that moment. 

What moved me most was that while I was extending invitations around the office, some colleagues inquired if I was feeling better after my difficult week. That level of empathy is invaluable. Occasionally, a leader needs to recharge their batteries from others.

### Word of the Week:
> **To Recharge your batteries:** To regain your energy or enthusiasm.
> *Usage: "Talking to *The Logistics Lead* really helped me recharge my batteries after a difficult week."*
